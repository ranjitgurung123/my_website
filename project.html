Biosphere Reserves

Biosphere Reserves: UNESCO's Man and the Biosphere Programme designates and manages biosphere reserves worldwide. These reserves serve as learning areas for sustainable development, balancing conservation with local economic and social needs. An exemplary project within this framework is the Mont Saint-Hilaire Biosphere Reserve in Quebec, Canada, which integrates conservation efforts with research, education, and community engagement.
